All Programs

We offer a variety of programs to help support our veterans. Our goal is to help veterans that then help other veterans, creating a cycle of growing, healing, and mentoring.

Reset

Peer support groups are a safe space where Veterans can discuss day-to-day problems with other Veterans who have experienced trauma

Rebuild

Peer support groups offer a safe space for discussing daily challenges and trauma with others who shared similar experiences.

Refocus

ReFocus uses photography and videography to address trauma through storytelling with hands-on workshops of camera basics, lighting, and conception.

ReEngage

ReEngage is a career development program for Veterans transitioning to new careers. This free workshop teaches skills like writing, reading, computer use, and interview prep.

Replant

Replant is a sustainable farming program with indoor gardens in community spaces. Veterans and community members gather weekly.
