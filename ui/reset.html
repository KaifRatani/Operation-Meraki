Reset
Welcome to ReSet, your personal haven for well-being and healing. Experience Trauma-Informed Yoga as a comforting embrace, tailored to your needs, fostering mindfulness and reclaiming control. Mindfulness and Meditation are not just practices; they're trusty sidekicks offering tools to navigate stress and enhance self-awareness. ReSet is a community where peer support groups provide connection, understanding, and camaraderie. Join us on this journey towards resilience, connection, and renewed well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a friend or family member?
Yes, family members are encouraged to join our programs as part of the healing process.
Do I have to pay anything?
No, all Operation Meraki programs are completely free for veterans and their families.
Do I need to bring any supplies?
No, all necessary equipment and supplies will be provided for the program.
What if I have no experience?
Our programs are designed for all experience levels, from beginners to advanced. No prior experience is necessary.
